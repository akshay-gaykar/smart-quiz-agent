AI-Powered Intelligence

Quiz Generation

  • Upload PDF study materials
  • AI extracts content and generates relevant questions
  • Configurable: question type, difficulty, count
  • Automatic explanations for each answer
  • Grade-level appropriate content

Answer Evaluation

  • Semantic understanding of short answers
  • Partial credit based on conceptual accuracy
  • Constructive feedback on incorrect responses
  • Auto-grading for all 6 question types

AI Tutor Chat

  • Conversational assistant for students
  • Context-aware help based on quiz topics
  • Explains concepts, not just answers
  • Persistent chat history per conversation

Analytics Intelligence

  • Performance trend analysis (improving/declining)
  • Weak subject identification
  • Topic mastery insights
  • Common mistake pattern detection
